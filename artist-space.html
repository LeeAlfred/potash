About the Artist

Author and Illustrator Lee Sampson, an experienced leader with over 20 years in various industries, uses a people-centric approach and creative vision to make leadership development accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Relatable and thought-provoking, I illustrate how to: create a positive team culture, demonstrate empathy, communicate effectively, build trust, coach, and mentor those around you.

I chose penguins as the characters as I wanted to be purposeful about highlighting that leadership is universal, transcending every other label we use to define/divide ourselves. My intention is for everyone to be able to see themselves in the illustration.
