About Potash

Potash is a platform born from the belief that art can help save humanity. It's a digital space connecting artists with communities to address needs both big and small, from transforming graffiti to art to raising awareness for important causes.

Our goal is to empower artists to make a positive difference in the world by providing providing a platform to showcase their work while directly supporting their community through charities they feel strongly about. We envision a global network where artists can work locally and globally, making their art more accessible and supporting meaningful change.
