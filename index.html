About Potash

Potash is a platform born from the belief that art can help save humanity. It's a digital space connecting artists with communities to address needs both big and small, from transforming graffiti to art to raising awareness for important causes.

Our goal is to empower artists to make a positive difference in the world by providing providing a platform to showcase their work while directly supporting their community through charities they feel strongly about. We envision a global network where artists can work locally and globally, making their art more accessible and supporting meaningful change.

Featured Artists

Lee Sampson

As an Author and Illustrator known for his engaging Penguin cartoons on leadership development, Lee Sampson embodies the spirit of artists making a difference. He believes in the transformative power of art and its ability to foster positive change, starting from within individuals and extending to communities.Inspired by the compassionate care his mother received from Mary Potter Hospice, Lee is dedicated to using his creative vision and the Potash platform to connect artists with meaningful causes, hoping to amplify their impact and celebrate their contributions
